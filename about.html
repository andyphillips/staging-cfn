About Us

Community-led conservation

We help local communities lead the protection of the ecosystems they call home.

Lasting conservation happens when local leadership, trusted partnerships and long-term investment come together — not through short-term projects.

Our Mission

Enabling stewardship across generations

We exist to enable communities to lead environmental stewardship while strengthening livelihoods, education and long-term resilience.

Our focus: restoring ecosystems, strengthening community capability, and building the conditions for stewardship to endure.

Our Model

A partnership platform

We operate as a partnership platform — bringing together community organisations, delivery NGOs, scientists, educators, corporates and foundations.

Together, we co-design multi-year programmes grounded in trust, transparency and shared accountability.

Our Approach

What makes us different

Five principles that shape every partnership we build.

Long-term by design

Multi-year programmes that build institutional capability — not short-term pilots.

Trusted partnerships

We invest heavily in relationship quality, transparency and accountability.

Community-led delivery

Communities shape priorities, solutions and governance, supported by technical expertise.

Practical delivery

Focus on what works on the ground — grounded in evidence and local knowledge.

Integrated impact

Environment, livelihoods, education and governance are designed together.
